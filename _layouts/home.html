the web’s leading online collaborative genealogy application
This site will eventually replace http://wiki.webtrees.net and the non-forum pages in https://www.webtrees.net.

Free & Open-Source

webtrees is open-source software, which means it’s constantly improved by a community of developers and costs $0 - yes, that means totally FREE! All you need to use it is a website with PHP and MySQL installed.

It works from standard GEDCOM files, and is therefore compatible with every major desktop application; and it aims to be efficient and effective by using the right combination of third-party tools, design techniques and open standards.

Collaborative

Create additional user accounts so you can build your tree together with your relatives around the world.

Customizable

Configure page modules, menus, charts, reports, and more. Give your site a little flair with different themes- choose from the six default or browse themes from the community.

International

Choose from over 60 languages and numerous surname conventions, such as surname-first order, matrilineal surname tradition, patronymic or matronymic surnames, and more.

Secure & Private

This is a VERY important aspect of putting your family history on the web. With webtrees your data is as secure as you want it to be. Privacy settings are flexible, and control is configurable at multiple levels.
